Glass Onion: A Knives Out Mystery

2022.Mystery/Crime.2h19m

plot

During the COVID-19 pandemic in May 2022, Miles Bron, the billionaire co-founder of the Manhattan technology company Alpha, decides to host a murder mystery weekend at his mansion, the Glass Onion, on his private island in Greece.

Upon arrival, Miles questions Blanc's presence, but allows him to stay.

Blanc overhears Peg confront Miles over a PR statement he wants Birdie to release and sees Duke watch Whiskey flirting with Miles on a bed.
